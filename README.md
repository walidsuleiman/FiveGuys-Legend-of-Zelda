**Legend of Zelda**\
W to move forward\
A to move left\
S to move back\
D to move right\
1 to equip wood sword\
2 to equip white sword\
3 to unequip swords\
N to use sword attack 1 in game\
N to select items in inventory\
E to take damage and die\
R to Reset\
M to mute\
Q to Quit\
B to use items\
Z to use boomerang\
C to open inventory\
L to activate darkmode\
Enter to Pause / Menu
Use the doors to go to another room
